About us

The Fleet Science platform is initiated by Conor and Sarah la Grue from Given Time. This is their story.

This seven-year exploration mission will see us circumnavigate the globe, a huge adventure in its own right! But this sailing expedition is way more than just a lap around the planet! We will be the lead vessel in a collaborative marine ecology research project, which will see the worlds cruising community work hand in hand with the worlds scientific community. We are creating a global network of free resource for marine expedition research - A first of its kind mission of “Exploration through Collaboration” - not crowd science but “Fleet Science!”

We also plan to open up our vessel, our home, to visiting environmentalists, researchers, academics and scientists and host them to further enable their research! All while we cross oceans and visit remote locations - again providing this hosting as a free resource to the science community. The oceans belong to all 7.6 billion of us humans here on Earth, but we are often made to feel like their health is a far away issue and just not our problem! But they belong to you too, so it’s truly everyone’s problem!

This crew have previously produced documentaries as both presenters and as contributors and worked in a high innovation, open technology, sharing, outreach and education innovation environment for the best part of a decade. They also have broad fundraising, open source project experience, key story telling skills and vital social media, photography, videography, production experience to help bring all this fleet science exploration through collaboration story to life!

To that end as well as launching and managing the Fleet Science project, the crew are also filming a supporting documentary series - “Sailing Given Time” its main aim to follow this entire adventure from the point where they sold their house and moved to a life afloat on SV Given Time - through to their global travel and adventures as they complete this seven-year mission. This expedition will take them to places of exceptional natural beauty and they will share the best and the worst of what they find in this multi season documentary series.

FAQ: Costs, Partners and Help

Costs? Yes lots of them! - we have invested everything to get this project started, so we very much welcome your support in whatever form it comes from; producing project assets and providing digital hosting and infrastructure to support in equipment for the expedition or primary funding!

If you are a company that would like to support our efforts with resource, knowhow, equipment or funding - then great! do please get in touch and we can get together to see how you could help.
